Features

Everything your team needs to ship

Engineered for engineering teams. No fluff, no bloat — just the tools that matter.

⚡

Sprint Boards

Kanban and scrum boards with drag-and-drop, swimlanes, and real-time sync across your whole team.

◷

Timeline & Gantt

Visual project timelines with dependency mapping, milestone tracking, and capacity planning built in.

◈

Velocity Analytics

Burndown charts, cycle time, and velocity metrics that actually help you improve — not just report.
